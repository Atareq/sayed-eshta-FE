Design a full multi-page Arabic RTL website for "السيد قشطة" (El Sayed Qeshta), a premium Egyptian dessert brand specializing in rice pudding (أرز بلبن), Om Ali (أم علي), and Qashtota (قشطوطة). The brand personality is warm, playful, nostalgic, and joyful.

---

BRAND IDENTITY:
- Primary Logo: Arabic calligraphy "السيد قشطة" in bold pink (#FC8CB0) on black — decorative, bubbly letterforms
- Mascot: A cute chubby 3D cartoon cow wearing round glasses, a cream knit scarf, brown overalls, and white sneakers — friendly and nerdy character named "السيد قشطة"
- Pattern: Organic swirling psychedelic pink-on-black pattern (like brain coral/maze) — used as texture/section divider
- Tone: Playful, warm, Egyptian colloquial, inviting, slightly retro

COLOR PALETTE:
- Flamingo Pink: #FC8CB0 (primary — text, highlights, CTAs)
- Royal Orange: #FE9A4E (secondary — warm accents, buttons)
- Moonstone Blue: #70C1CD (tertiary — cool contrast, tags)
- Misty Rose: #FAE7E8 (background tints, cards)
- Black: #000000 (backgrounds, contrast sections)
- White: #FFFFFF (clean sections)

TYPOGRAPHY:
- Arabic Display Font: Lalezar or Tajawal Black — bold, rounded, playful
- Arabic Body Font: Tajawal Regular — clean and readable
- Direction: RTL (Right to Left) — all layouts must be mirrored for Arabic

PAGE 1 — STORY / REGISTRATION PAGE (Landing/Onboarding)

PURPOSE: Collect user data (name, phone, city, favorite dessert) before entering the site. This is a fun story-driven onboarding experience — NOT a boring form.

LAYOUT CONCEPT:
- Full-screen dark (#000) background
- The pink swirl pattern fills the bottom 30% as a footer texture
- Large logo "السيد قشطة" centered at top in pink calligraphy
- Mascot cow character appears on the right side, large and friendly
- A speech bubble from the cow says: "أهلاً! أنا السيد قشطة 👋 قبل ما تدخل عالم الحلو، قولي عنك شوية!"
- Below: animated story cards that appear one by one (like Instagram stories UX) telling the brand origin story — 3-4 frames
- After story: registration form slides up with:
  * Input: الاسم الأول
  * Input: رقم الموبايل
  * Dropdown: المدينة
  * Multi-select pills: إيه أكتر حلو بتحبه؟ (أرز بلبن / أم علي / قشطوطة / كلهم!)
  * Big pink CTA button: "ادخل عالم القشطة! 🍮"
- Micro-copy below button: "بياناتك معانا أمانة — مش هنزعلك بإيميلات كتير 😄"

STYLE NOTES for Page 1:
- Dark and festive feel — like a dessert party invitation
- Cow mascot should feel like it's "welcoming" the visitor
- Speech bubbles in pink with black text
- Form inputs: rounded pill shape, pink border, white bg
- Animated entrance of each story card (slide from right)
